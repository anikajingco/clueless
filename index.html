Clueless

The movie begins with a peak into Cher’s life: bright, affluent, and fun. Her father is a lawyer and it is established that they have a close relationship. We get a peak into Cher’s life as we follow her day and time in high school.

Cher begins her quest in helping others, starting with matchmaking teachers Mr. Hall and Ms. Geist. She recruits best friend Dionne to help her and–while an attempt to get a better grade–Cher still wants the best for these teachers. Things look up when they start becoming enamoured with each other.

After the success of her matchmaking services, Cher believes she can help adorable and naive new-girl Tai become acquainted with their school and all of its workings. The girls decide to give Tai a makeover to assimilate her into their world and friend group.

One way of getting Tai out of her comfort zone is taking her to a party. Here, she and Elton share a moment before she gets hurt. Elton, in turn, takes Cher home where he tries to make a move on her. Cher refuses and walks home, only to be robbed and later picked up by Josh. Elton and Tai date briefly before ultimately breaking up.

Christian moves to their school and Cher is instantly infatuated with him. She tries to draw his attention and the two become good friends. During this time, Tai is getting increasingly more popular and starts to have feelings for Josh.

Cher begins to distance herself from Tai. She busies herself by organizing a donation for the Pismo Beach Relief. Cher’s character development is evident here as she is shown to be less selfish and more well rounded. At Travis’ skateboard event, her and Tai apologize and makeup.

The movie ends with Josh and Cher admitting their mutual feelings for each other and attending Ms. Geist’s and Mr. Hall’s wedding along with Dionne, Murray, Tai, and Travis.
